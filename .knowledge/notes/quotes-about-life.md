# Quotes About Life

# Quotes About Life

A collection of meaningful quotes about life from various thinkers and artists.

## Emerson on Purpose

> **"The purpose of life is not to be happy. It is to be useful, to be honorable, to be compassionate, to have it make some difference that you have lived and lived well."**
> — Ralph Waldo Emerson

Emerson challenges the modern obsession with personal happiness, suggesting that true fulfillment comes from contributing to others and living with integrity. It's a call to shift focus from self-gratification to service and character.

## Lennon on Plans

> **"Life is what happens when you're busy making other plans."**
> — John Lennon

A reminder to stay present. We often get so caught up in preparing for the future that we miss the moments happening right now. The unexpected, unplanned parts of life often become the most meaningful memories.

## Dr. Seuss on Perspective

> **"Don't cry because it's over, smile because it happened."**
> — Dr. Seuss

Simple wisdom about gratitude and perspective. Rather than mourning endings, celebrate the experience itself. It's about choosing to be thankful for what was, instead of sad about what is no longer.
